It was in 2006, with little capital but a pocketful of belief, our CEO, Raisul Kabir, started Brain Station 23, a software company, right after graduating from BUET. The new company initially focused on the international market, with the local market added in 2010. Since then, the company has shown continuous growth and currently employs over 800+ professionals. Brain Station 23 is now not only an established name in Bangladesh but also in countries like the USA,  UAE, Malaysia, UK, Netherlands, Denmark, Japan, Norway, Sweden, Germany, Canada, Switzerland, Turkey, etc.
